SnakeTheGame

This is just another Snake remake!

About the game:

Your objective is simple, control the snake in order to eat as many apples as posible without accidentally eating yourself... cause that's kinda dumb to be honest.

In tihs version of the game the borders will allow you to appear on the opposite side of the board, so use that to your advantage!

Main Items:

Snake:

Move the snake to eat apples, avoid your own body!

Apple:

Apples spawn randomly, eating it increases the snakes length and score.

Golden Apple:

Bonus item, increases the snakes length and score by 3.
Disappears after a few seconds.
